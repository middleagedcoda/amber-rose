The Origin File

She never
apologized for herself.

"I was called a slut for being sexually empowered. So I started a movement. I walked. And millions walked with me."
1983
Born in Philadelphia · Italian, Cape Verdean, Irish, Scottish
Amber Rose Levonchuck grew up in South Philadelphia. Her mother was a model, her father a musician. She learned early that being different meant being targeted — and that the only response was unapologetic self-ownership.
2009
The World Meets Amber Rose
Burst onto the scene with her iconic buzzcut, bold style, and unflinching confidence. Became a muse for the fashion world and a tabloid fixture — but she was always more than the headlines. She was building something.
2015
SlutWalk Los Angeles · Global Movement
After being shamed for her sexuality, she organized the first SlutWalk in Los Angeles. Thousands marched against victim blaming. The movement spread to over 100 cities worldwide. She didn't just talk about change — she organized it.
2018
How to Be a Bad Bitch · New York Times Bestseller
Her book became a #1 New York Times bestseller. Part memoir, part guide to self-empowerment. "A bad bitch is a woman who refuses to apologize for who she is." The book sold out instantly. A second printing followed.
2019
Amber Rose Foundation · Advocacy & Education
Founded her foundation to support women's rights, body positivity, and anti-bullying education. She turned personal experience into institutional change. The SlutWalk movement became a permanent part of her legacy.
2023
24M+ Instagram · Mogul · Media Personality
Actress, model, entrepreneur, activist. She became a mogul without ever changing who she was. Endorsements, partnerships, her own production company, and a voice that remains one of the most trusted in pop culture.
2025+
New Projects · Always Unapologetic
New television projects, continued activism, and a growing empire. The woman who was once shamed for her sexuality now mentors the next generation of women to own their power. The movement never stopped — it only grew.
